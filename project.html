Weather Warning App

The Weather Warning app was a prototype I made in Adobe XD. It is made for people travelling to countries known for their extreme weather. It will send the user a warning when it is to dangerous. It will show routes towards the nearby hospitals, hotels and airports to help the user stay save. When there is no warning needed it can be used to see what the weather forecast is.
